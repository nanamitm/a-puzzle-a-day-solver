A-Puzzle-A-Day Solver

This is an unofficial solver of DragonFjord's A-Puzzle-A-Day and similar puzzles. You can find one of solutions to the specified day's layout.

Note: If you use this tool, you may lose the joy of solving puzzles on your own. Be careful!

* The weekday option is only valid for "WeekDay Calendar Puzzle".

Tip You can prefill by URL. Example: ?date=2026-01-01&type=dragonfjord. Full help: README
